## About NAB Wecamp Program

The WeCamp Program is a specialized early-career initiative launched by the NAB Innovation Centre Vietnam (NAB Vietnam). It is specifically designed to empower women and career switchers to break into the technology sector, addressing the gender gap in the IT industry.  The program serves as a bridge for those who have the curiosity and ambition to build a career in tech but may lack a formal background in the field.
